>
> Built with enterprise-grade security controls aligned with ISO 27001, GDPR, and SOC 2 standards:
> - ✅ Encryption at rest (AWS KMS) and in transit (TLS 1.3)
> - ✅ Role-based access control (RBAC) with least-privilege enforcement
> - ✅ Multi-factor authentication (MFA) required
> - ✅ Immutable audit logs with 7-year retention
> - ✅ Automated evidence collection 24/7
> - ✅ One-click compliance reports (ISO 27001, GDPR, SOC 2)
>
> **Audit-ready from launch.** Formal certifications in progress (Q3-Q4 2026).

### For Customer Conversations

**When customers ask: "Are you ISO 27001 certified?"**

✅ **Correct response:**
> "We're compliance-ready at launch with all technical controls in place according to ISO 27001 standards. Our formal ISO 27001 certification audit is scheduled for Q3 2026. The platform is audit-ready today, and we can provide detailed control documentation upon request."

**When customers ask: "Do you have SOC 2?"**

✅ **Correct response:**
> "We're pursuing SOC 2 Type 1 (Q3 2026) and Type 2 (Q4 2026). Our platform is built with SOC 2 Trust Services Criteria from day 1, including automated evidence collection. We can share our control matrix and current compliance posture during evaluation."

**When customers ask: "Are you GDPR compliant?"**

✅ **Correct response:**
> "Yes. GDPR is a legal requirement, not a certification. We implement all required technical and organizational measures: data encryption, access controls, audit trails, right to erasure workflows, and breach notification procedures. We can provide our GDPR compliance documentation."

## ❌ TRÁNH: Messaging Sai

### ❌ Không nói:
- "We're ISO 27001 certified" (chưa có certificate)
- "We have SOC 2" (chưa có report)
- "Fully certified at launch" (misleading)
- "Compliance coming soon" (technical controls đã có)

### ❌ Không oversell:
- "We'll be certified in 6 months" (audit timeline có thể thay đổi)
- "Guaranteed certification by Q3" (audit outcome không đảm bảo 100%)

### ❌ Không undersell:
- "We're not compliant yet" (technical controls đã đầy đủ)
- "Just starting compliance work" (đã build-in từ đầu)
